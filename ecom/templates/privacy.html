2. Refund & Return Policy

At Angels we ensure all our products are genuine and hygienically packed.

Returns

Due to hygiene reasons, cosmetics and skincare products cannot be returned once opened/used. Returns are accepted only if:

  • Wrong product delivered
  • Damaged product received

You must notify us within 48 hours of delivery with unboxing video/photos.

Refunds

Once the return is approved, refund will be processed within 7–10 working days to your original payment method. Shipping charges are non-refundable.

Exchange

Exchange possible only if the wrong or damaged item was sent.

3. Shipping Policy

Delivery Locations

We ship across India through trusted courier partners.

Processing Time

Orders are processed within 24–48 hours (business days). You will receive tracking details once shipped.

Delivery Time

  • Metro cities: 3–5 working days
  • Other locations: 5–7 working days
  • Remote areas: 7–10 working days

Shipping Charges

  • Free shipping on orders above ₹1000
  • Standard shipping charges apply for orders below the limit

Delays

Delays may occur due to courier issues, strikes, natural calamities, or festive rush. We will keep you updated in such cases.
