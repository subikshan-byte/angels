About Us

Since 1982, Angels Fashion has been at the heart of beauty & fashion in Anna Nagar, Chennai.

Where it all Began

From a single nail polish to a world of nail care & beauty – that’s the Angels journey. Founded by Mr. Suresh Kumar, with the vision of creating more than a store – a place that sets trends and inspires beauty. At Angels, beauty has no boundaries.

Built on relationships and trusted by thousands of salons, beauticians & loyal retail customers for decades, Angels became pioneers in Anna Nagar – among the very first AC showrooms dedicated to beauty & fashion.

Our Milestone

Angels Fashion reached a big milestone by opening the first AC showroom in Anna Nagar for beauty & fashion. Over the years, we expanded into fashion & bridal jewellery, partnering with leading national and global brands.

With Abhishek as the CEO today, Angels Fashion continues to build on this legacy – delivering timeless style and trusted beauty products to every customer.
